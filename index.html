Our Motive

Set up in January 2022, we are a collective of gamers who decided to give the community we cherish the facilities to enjoy what they love most. Wether that is state of the art gaming PCs, the newest consoles, or top of the range VR simulators.

Our Goals

We want to provide our visitors the ability to hone their skills in their respective area. We already have a strong collective of e-sports teams that are already chasing their dreams. If you're visiting for the social side of things, we got you. This is the perfect place for it.
